MentE

MentE is an app built for educational organizations with mentorship programs. Mentees sign up for the app, choose from a list of pre-approved mentors, and get matched with one by our algorithm. Mentors sign up for the app to give back without busy work and red tape. The app tracks the meetings between pairings, allowing administrators to quickly notice high-quality mentors for repeat involvement and trim low-quality mentors.


Key features include:

  • Mentee Registration and Mentor Selection: Mentees can sign up and choose from a list of pre-approved mentors
  • Automated Matching Algorithm: An algorithm matches mentees with mentors based on their skills and selections matches.
  • Meeting Tracking: The platfrom tracks meetings between mentors and mentees, enabling administrators to monitor engagement quality.
  • Mentor Performance Evaluation: Administrators can identitfy high-quality mentors for repeat involvement and address underperformance.
  • Tailwind Integration: The UI is styled with tailwind to ensure a clean, modern, and mobile-friendly design.

Technology Stack:

Backend: Ruby on Rails
Frontend: HTML, JavaScript, CSS, Tailwind
Database: PostgreSQL
Additional tools: Docker, ESLint, Rubocop, Azure
Version control: GitHub
